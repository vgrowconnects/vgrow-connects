General Refund Policy

Vgrow Connects strives to ensure the satisfaction of our customers. However, as a distributor of third-party services, our refund policies are aligned with those of the service providers we represent. Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

1. Cancellation Before Installation

If you cancel your service request before the service installation, you may be eligible for a full refund of any upfront payments made to Vgrow Connects.

2. Service Issues

If there is a verifiable issue with the service installation or activation that is directly attributable to Vgrow Connects, we will work to resolve the issue. In rare cases where resolution is not possible, a refund may be issued.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Fees associated with the processing of services are non-refundable. This includes (but is not limited to):

  • Installation charges
  • Activation fees
  • Administrative costs
